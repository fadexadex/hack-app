## Design Context

### Users
People who want to preview furniture or decor inside their own environment before committing to a purchase or layout change. They are usually checking fit, proportion, and visual balance inside a room with a phone in hand.

### Brand Personality
Confident, tactile, editorial. The experience should feel like a curated showroom rather than a developer demo.

### Aesthetic Direction
Warm gallery tones, soft atmospheric gradients, and serif-led typography paired with a clean sans-serif body. The interface should feel calm and premium, not futuristic or neon-heavy.

### Design Principles
- Make placement feel concrete and trustworthy.
- Keep the main AR action obvious at all times.
- Let the 3D object stay visually central while support guidance stays nearby.
- Prefer warmth, texture, and restraint over cold “tech demo” styling.
